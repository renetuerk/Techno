Technology & Innovation Office — Organisation & Mission
Organisationseinheit · Namensvorschlag
Technology & Innovation Office
TIO

"Technology" benennt den Kernauftrag direkt. "Innovation" signalisiert den strategischen Anspruch nach außen. "Office" positioniert die Einheit als Querschnitts- und Koordinierungsfunktion innerhalb von PM/KPZ Fire Safety & Security — mit Anbindung an Produktmanagement International.

Organigramm
Mission Statement
"

The Technology & Innovation Office enables PM/KPZ Fire Safety & Security to systematically identify, evaluate and integrate new technologies into products, product management processes and internal service — creating a structural competitive advantage in fire safety and security technology.

We serve PM/KPZ Fire Safety & Security as our primary domain, act as a cross-functional enabler across the organisation, and coordinate innovation intelligence within the Securitas Group's international product management.
